Instructions

  • Click the window to focus on it if the controls don't work.
  • Press the key to activate thrust.
  • Press the key to turn right.
  • Press the key to turn left.
  • Shooting small asteroids earns you 100 points.
  • Shooting medium-sized asteroids earns you 50 points.
  • Shooting large asteroids earns you 20 points.
  • You have 3 lives. You lose a life when you get hit by an asteroid. When all 3 lives have been used up, the game ends.
